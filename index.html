How It Works

Four stages, infinite secrets. Here's your roadmap.

🔍

Explore

Browse the website and look for anything unusual — hidden text, odd comments, and secret elements.

🧩

Solve

Use the clues you find to answer puzzles and enter the correct secret codes.

🚪

Unlock

Correct answers unlock the next stage. Progress is saved so you never lose your place.

🏆

Escape!

Complete all rooms to reach the Final Escape Screen and claim your victory!

What Is This?

A new kind of web experience that rewards curiosity.

🌐

The Website IS the Puzzle

Unlike traditional games, Escape The Website hides clues inside the actual HTML, CSS, and JavaScript of the page. Right-click, inspect, and dig deeper to find them.

🔒

Progressive Room Unlocking

Each room is locked until you solve the previous one. Your progress is saved in your browser — so you can take breaks and come back anytime.

⏱️

Race Against the Clock

A timer starts the moment you enter Room 1 and keeps running across all rooms. Solve faster to set a new personal best time!

🤓

Learn While You Play

Along the way you'll naturally learn about HTML structure, browser dev tools, CSS tricks, and how websites are built — all through fun exploration.
